We Are All Divine Creators

Here at Empress Moon Art, we help you see the light you have within.

Namaste, I'm Dalia.

A little about myself, I'm from the beautiful border town of El Paso, Texas. Raised by young parents and a village that is my family, we are on the road to building generational health and wealth. As a human collective, we have been led to believe our dreams are limited and dependent on our ethnicity, religion, age, and barriers. I created this page not only to display my creative expressions but to challenge that limited belief and show that more is possible with the right tools.

Manifestation has been a huge trend as of late, but I believe there is a component that is often missed when trying to achieve that goal. That missing piece is emotion. We can say what we desire out loud 1,000 times, but if your energy does not match the feeling of that goal, it will make it a bit difficult to get there. Once we learn that how we feel is linked to what our reality reflects, we would make the conscious decision every day to rewrite that narrative.

Please, take some time and watch this insightful video from Dr. Joe Dispenza that has changed my life, you deserve that opportunity. After, on my contact page, feel free to write whatever you are holding onto that needs to be released for your highest good, or whatever you feel called to write.

Thank you for taking the time to view my creation, and taking some time for yourself.
